Make Humanity A Priority

Whatever the mind of a man can conceive and believe, it can achieve.

The Promotion and support of social activities through NGO obviously need a vision, which forms a basic for action. The vision can identify priority areas which are deemed important either for their intrinsic merit in primitive way or for its potential applied value in doing so, Hands for humanity has set criteria with a nucleus of social work like health awareness, environmental problem, women emancipation etc. By way of individual discussion and group meeting from the very beginning Hans Foundation thinking that the expertise acquired by the, must be utilized for benefit of society, since its inception Hands for humanity has strived to further cause of the social problems by way of the individual.
